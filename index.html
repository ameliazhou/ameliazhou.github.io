currently

Researching the intersection of movement and poetic practices, and how these speak in dialogue to produce emergent and transformative selves.

She is developing a poetics based on somatic and movement processes to investigate her ongoing preoccupation with the performativity of race and femininities.

~As part of a MA Creative Practice (Transdisciplinary) at Trinity Laban, London (2019-20). Leverhulme Arts Scholar recipient.
